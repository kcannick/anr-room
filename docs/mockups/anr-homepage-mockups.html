Makin' It Magazine · The A&R Room

One page, scroll to sections, two states. The site reads its own status: live → red banner + a screenshot of the YouTube stream + "watch on YouTube" links, then "get in to vote." Between shows → explainer, next session (with Register, not just a reminder — the real conversion), leaderboard, winners, submit funnel. The voting app is a second-screen companion — people watch on one device and vote on another (especially at live events and watch-alongs), so the app stays clean and never fights the audio they're already hearing. All data-driven; you run sessions, the page updates itself.
